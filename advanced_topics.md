The remaining four sections are optional tracks that build on the core. You can choose one, two, or more depending on your interests and goals. They are:

- **Methods, Evidence, and Analysis**  
  (data, research, and analytical skills for evidence-based public work)

- **Public Finance and Budgeting**  
  (money, taxation, budgets, and fiscal decision-making in government)

- **Applied Policy Domains**  
  (specific policy areas such as environment, health, and digital government)

- **Comparative, International, and Nonprofit Administration**  
  (how public administration works in other countries, in international development, and in nonprofit/third-sector organizations)
